Tutorial: Query

You can already ask the first questions on top of your dataset, either by selecting it from the top right list of datasets, or by clicking on the "ACCESS" and "QUERY" button under your profile.

For the "cocktails" dataset you could ask for example: "What is a Margarita?" or "What are the ingredients of Margarita?":

and

Note that for now there is no contextual information attached to the answers, like labels, descriptions, external links, maps, images and videos. How this can be achieved will be shown in Part 6.

The dataset contains some popular cocktails like: Margarita, Sex on the Beach, Long Island Iced Tea and Grasshopper.

The results that you achieve may not be satisfying. To train the machine learning model to fit your dataset check the next section.
